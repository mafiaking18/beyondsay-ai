What is BeyondSay?

BeyondSay is an AI-powered chatbot created by Beyond Co. to support our clients with quick, helpful answers — anytime, day or night. It’s built using OpenAI’s ChatGPT technology, which means it can understand and respond to questions in a natural, human-like way.

We’ve trained BeyondSay to reflect our tone of voice and focus on the things clients ask us most — from file submissions and timelines to service details and project support. It’s like having one of our team members ready to chat, even when we’re offline.

While it can’t replace real conversations for more complex issues, BeyondSay is a great first step to getting the info you need, fast.
